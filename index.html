Location

Our company is located in the heart of Lithuania, a small country, in a small but extraordinarily beautiful town of Molėtai.

Growth

We are a growing transporter. Currently we have 72 trucks – 50 curtainside and 22 frigo trucks. Additionally, we employ several smaller hauliers to work under our name. To this day the fleet of hired hauliers amounts to over 170 trucks.

Dedication

However, we think big, and no task is too daunting for us ! With each passing day our company grows and is being recognized by more and more suppliers and manufacturers of Fresh and frozen goods.

European operations

Fresh and frozen goods deliveries

Constant deliveries of fresh and frozen goods in Central Europe. Trailer temperature range from -25 to +25 Celsius. Deliveries of frozen meat and vegetables between Netherlands, Belgium, Germany, Italy, France, Spain, Austria, Czech Republic, Slovenia, Slovakia and Hungary. Most trailers equipped with 33 euro pallets if the customer requires pallet exchange

Oversized loads

Network of subcontrators for deliveries of oversized loads with Mega, Road-train and platform trailers. We are a partner of several Germany spedition companies for deliveries of military vehicles and equipment to Lithuania and Latvia.

Cargo carriage in Western Europe

Daily steel, paper, construction materials and frozen goods deliveries in Benelux, France and Germany

FTL and LTL

FTL and LTL network across all EU thanks to extensive partner network and warehousing agreements

Warehousing services

  • Preparing and issuing Export Declaration (EX) for any EU natural or juridical persons
  • Closing EX and opening of TIR carnet
  • Preparing load’s documents according to client’s requirements
  • Sorting the load, packing, marking, weighing, and extra fastening
  • Temperature cargo storage
